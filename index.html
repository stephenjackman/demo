      Report. I’ve come across some things in my site I wish to work on. Initially, the bar displaying links to different pages was considerably small. Upon reflection and advice from my group mate, I see it makes more sense for the bar to take up more space on the page. This balances some elements out and creates a sort of symmetry. I would argue the balance and separation of content is well done. Of course, there isn’t much content within the page right now but I believe I categorize it well so viewers understand what they’re absorbing. I believe the content, while sparse, speaks for itself so the presentation of the whole page simply draws people in. I remain confident in my color scheme as of right now. Blue, as mentioned in class, is a very accessible color to some audiences. It is neutral and easy on the eyes yet still attractive and sleek. All in all it is a rough start but it is fair. I do not believe I have taken any great risks that have immensely affected the rest of the work I have set out to do and am confident a decent webpage will be completed by the end of the term.
